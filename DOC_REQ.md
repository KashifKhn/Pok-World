### **1. Document Structure**

The report must include the following sections in order:

1. **Abstract**

   - On page #01 only
   - Maximum 1 page

2. **Introduction**

   - Around 2 pages minimum

3. **Motivation**

   - Maximum half page
   - Can be written in bullet points
   - Answer why this field? What is the main reason?

4. **Literature Review / Related Work**

   - Include **10 related works**
   - Should be presented **in table form**

5. **Problem Statement**

   - Half page
   - Bullet points

6. **Contribution / Simulations**

   - Include **mockups**
   - Add **captions**
   - Highlight **novelty of your work**
   - Attach screenshots and explain each screenshot

7. **Conclusion & Future Work**

   - Mention possible future extensions

8. **References**

   - Same order as the related work

---

### **2. Overall Document Requirements**

- Total length: **10 pages**
- **Less than 18% plagiarism**
- Maintain consistent **font type, size, and formatting**
- Everything written must be _according to your knowledge_
